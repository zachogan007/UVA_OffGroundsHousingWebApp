Our mission is to provide University of Virginia students with an easy-to-use, free service for them to explore and discover various housing options across the Charlottesville area. This centralized source contains directional services, listings from all over the Charlottesville area, as well as reviews from previous tenants.

This centralized source contains:

  • GPS/direction service
  • Reviews from previous tenants
  • Housing information of the listings
  • Centralized calendar of housing events

    • With this service, we hope that UVA students will be able to make well informed decisions and that it alleviates some of the pressure that comes with finding stable housing.
